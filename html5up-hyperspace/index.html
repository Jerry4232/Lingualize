Who we are

We are a group of passionate students from UCI majoring in computer science-related fields, aiming to use our knowledge to help improve the world. As international students, we understand the challenges of speaking in a non-familiar language. Our goal is to help people gain confidence in English speaking, encouraging them to speak more comfortably and fluently.

WHAT DO WE DO

We are currently building on an app called Lingualize, which is aiming to help people to study English in an more efficient way.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to help people improve in English Listening and Speaking. Therefore, we decide to devleop Lingualize to assist people to get use to life in a new country with new language. Our app would use real life scences and incoprporate some AI characters to motivate people to speak English in a more interactive and interesting way.

APP FEATURES

Our app have incorporate diverse features to assist people learn English in an more intersting way and helping them to improve confidence in daily speaking

AI Speech Recognition

Integrates speech-to-text technology to help users practice and improve their pronunciation by comparing their speech to native speakers.

Grammar Correction API

Uses AI to identify and correct grammatical mistakes in real-time, providing users with detailed explanations to improve their writing.

Real-Time Translation API

Provides seamless real-time translation of text and speech, making it easier for users to communicate in any language while learning English.

Personalized Learning Path

Offers personalized study plans based on the user's current level, progress, and learning style, using machine learning to adapt the curriculum.
